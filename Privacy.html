Privacy Policy

At MOVIE DAKHI, your privacy is a top priority. This document outlines the types of information collected and how we use it.

1. Log Files

MOVIE DAKHI follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, MOVIE DAKHI uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content.

3. Third Party Privacy Policies

MOVIE DAKHI's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.

4. Children's Information

We do not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately.
