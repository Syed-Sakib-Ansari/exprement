Detailed Plot Summary

Welcome back everyone! Today, we are diving deep into the dark, tech driven world of a relentless cyber thriller where family loyalty triggers an intense war against an all seeing corporate empire. We meet Toby, a quiet, brilliant teenage hacker who lives in the shadow of his accomplished older brother, Leo, a leading software engineer at the powerful OmniCorp tech conglomerate. The family's life is permanently shattered when Leo suddenly vanishes without a trace, and the corporation coldheartedly labels him a fugitive thief. Refusing to believe the official lies, Toby utilizes Leo's hidden bedroom terminal to hack into the corporate mainframe, inadvertently stumbling upon an absolute nightmare. He uncovers a secret, illegal AI surveillance program called Little Brother, capable of predicting and controlling human behavior by spying on every citizen. Toby realizes Leo was targeted because he tried to destroy it. Now, with deadly corporate mercenaries tracking Toby's digital footprint, he must flee into the urban underworld. Armed with his tech wits, Toby races against time to broadcast the hidden files globally, rescue his brother, and dismantle the totalitarian system.
